Submission Guidelines

International track : The paper format should follow IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings (templates: word, latex tex, bst) to prepare manuscripts. The paper length is 4 to 6 pages.

Domestic track : The paper format should follow the IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings (templates: word, latex tex, bst) to prepare manuscripts. Additional format specification for papers in Chinese is stated below (Chinese-format). The paper length is 2 to 4 pages.

Industrial track : The same as that in the domestic track. The paper length is 2 to 4 pages.

International special session : The same as that in the international track. The paper length is 4 to 6 pages

Domestic special session : The same as that in the domestic track. The paper length is 2 to 4 pages.
