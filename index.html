About TAAI 2020

The 25th International Conference on Technologies and Applications of Artificial Intelligence (TAAI2020) is an annual leading conference on artificial intelligence and is now a firmly established event in the AI community in Taiwan. It is mainly sponsored by Taiwanese Association for Artificial Intelligence (TAAI) and hosted by National Central University, which is located in Taoyuan City, Taiwan. This year we plan to invite more than three important scholars to give keynote speeches. Share the most cutting-edge AI research results. The keynote speakers of this conference over the years are very famous scholars and experts in academia and industry. From the background of the speakers, we can also see the characteristics of this conference in pursuit of excellence and international connection.
