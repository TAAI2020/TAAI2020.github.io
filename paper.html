Call for Papers

TAAI 2020 consists of three kinds of tracks: international, domestic and industrial. Papers on academic researches and industrial applications of artificial intelligence and related areas are desired. We also welcome experienced scholars to organize special sessions to detail specific topics. TAAI 2020 is open to international participants. It provides a very good opportunity for scholars interested in AI to gather together in the capital of Taiwan, sharing new results and promoting cooperation.
